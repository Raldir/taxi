Introduction

We present a system for taxonomy construction that got the first place in all subtasks of the SemEval 2016 challenge on Taxonomy Extraction Evaluation. Our simple yet efficient approach is based on ISA relations extracted with Hearst-style lexico-syntactic patterns from large corpora and substring inclusion. We rely on focused crawling to create custom corpora of each domain. F-measure of the golden standard evaluation of our method achieves up to 0.39 on golden standard evaluation and precision up to 0.90 on the on manual evaluation. Our experiments show that the method performs well across different domains and languages. Only a small manual labour is needed to adapt it to a new domain or language.

Taxonomy Induction Method

Our approach to taxonomy induction consist of four steps. First, we crawl domain-specific corpora based on terminology of the target domain. Second, candidate hypernyms are extracted based on substrings and lexico-syntactic patterns. These candidates are subsequently pruned on a per word basis.
